About Sitosis Minecraft

Sitosis is a private, survival Minecraft server. We are looking to build a community of mature Minecraft players; players that share a common interest in a server that abides by the following general precepts:

  • Vanilla: No game-altering modifications or plugins should be employed.

    • Sitosis only uses logging and rollback mods to aid in the identification of thieves, griefers, and cheaters and repair any damage done by them.

    • Players enjoy Minecraft in its unaltered form and do not employ client-side hacks or cheats which give advantages to some and ruin the immersion for others.

  • Mature: The community should value maturity in its players above all else.

    • Thieves, griefers, and cheaters are dealt with swiftly; all bans are permanent.

    • There is no age restriction on membership, but immature and obnoxious behavior from players of any age will result in expulsion if not corrected after sufficient warning.

  • Fun: A friendly environment of co-operation, comradery, and helpfulness should be fostered, in which players of all styles can feel welcomed and comfortable.

    • Whether you're a team-player who wants to get involved in massive community undertakings, or just want to find your own remote spot to build alone, you're welcome at Sitosis!
