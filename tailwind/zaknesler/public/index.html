Out of your comfort zone
June 2, 2018

When you’re a developer, there are certain things that are ingrained into your mind. You don’t necessarily want them there, but they’re hard to ignore. This is why every new idea in the world of development comes as a shock.

I started my interest in web development when I was in middle school; I am a high school senior as of now, and I must say that it was a roller coaster. Never had I once thought I’d make it this far. The throwbacks to days of reading, and not understanding, guides online remind me of how foreign programming truly is to an average person.

Learning a programming language, or the concept of computer programming in general, is something that you have to slowly get yourself into. To be able to read, and more importantly, to be able to understand code, is a developed skill that improves over time. It is also a skill that takes time to even get a grasp of, let alone master.
